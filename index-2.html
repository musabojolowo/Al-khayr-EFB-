🏆 Tournament Rules

1. Tournament Format

  • 20 Teams – Single Round Robin League (19 matchdays, 190 total matches)
  • Top 8 teams qualify for the Knockout Stage.

2. League Points

  • Win = 3 Points · Draw = 1 Point · Loss = 0 Points
  • Ranking: Points → Goal Difference → Goals Scored

3. Knockout Stage

  • Quarter-finals: 1st vs 8th, 2nd vs 7th, 3rd vs 6th, 4th vs 5th
  • Semi-finals → Final
  • Drawn knockout matches: Extra Time → Penalty Shoot-out

4. Match Settings

  • Duration: 8 Minutes · Extra Time: ON · Penalties: ON
  • Substitutions: 6 · Injuries: OFF

5. Disconnection Rules

  • Disconnect within first 4 minutes → replay.
  • Intentional disconnect while losing → 3–0 loss.

6. Registration

  • Entry Fee: ₦500 · Champion: ₦7,000 · Runner-up: ₦3,000

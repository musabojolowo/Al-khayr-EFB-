🏆 AL-KHAYR EFB TOURNAMENT RULES

1. Tournament Format

  • 32 Teams
  • Straight Knockout Format

Tournament Stages:

  • Round of 32
  • Round of 16
  • Quarter-finals
  • Semi-finals
  • Final

All matches are played over one leg only.

If a knockout match ends in a draw:

  • Extra Time will be played.
  • If still tied, the winner will be decided by Penalty Shootout.

2. Match Settings

  • Match Duration: 10 Minutes
  • Extra Time: ON
  • Penalties: ON
  • Substitutions: 6
  • Injuries: OFF

Players must use the official tournament settings for every match.

3. Disconnection Rules

If a match disconnects:

  • 0 to 5 minutes: Replay the full 10-minute match.
  • 5 to 10 minutes: Replay an 8-minute match.
  • 10 to 20 minutes: Replay a 7-minute match.
  • 20 to 30 minutes: Replay a 6-minute match.
  • 30 to 50 minutes: Replay a 5-minute match.
  • 50 to 70 minutes: Replay only the first half (5 minutes).

From 70 to 90 minutes:

  • If the player who was winning disconnects, replay only the remaining portion as agreed by the tournament organizers.
  • If the player who was losing disconnects, the current result stands and the winning player advances.

If a player intentionally quits while losing, the opponent will be awarded an additional 3 goals.

4. Match Deadlines

  • Every fixture must be completed before the announced deadline.
  • Failure by both players to play the match may result in a decision by the tournament organizers.
  • If only one player is active and the opponent is unavailable, the active player may be awarded a 3–0 victory after verification.
  • Claims must be made before the deadline with evidence where required.

5. Match Results

  • The winner must post a screenshot of the full-time result in the tournament WhatsApp group.
  • Tag an admin when posting the result.
  • Tournament records and brackets will only be updated after verification.

6. Player Conduct

  • Respect all players and tournament administrators.
  • No insults, harassment, abusive language, or arguments.
  • Any form of cheating or misconduct may result in:
    • Match forfeiture
    • Point deduction (if applicable)
    • Suspension
    • Disqualification
  • Fair play is expected throughout the tournament.

7. Tournament Decisions

The tournament organizers reserve the right to make the final decision on any dispute or situation not covered by these rules.
